# Contract Drafting and Review Guidance for China Law

Apply the San Guan Si Bu Fa method from T/CAB 0121-2021 to draft, review, and prioritize contract issues under a China-law lens.

## What It Does

- Reviews contracts through macro transaction structure, meso contract structure, and micro clause wording.
- Produces structured review memos, drafting outlines, clause rewrites, and signing checklists.
- Balances transaction promotion, risk control, and practical legal communication.
- Adds citation rules tied to the contract drafting and review quote library.

## When to Use It

- Review this contract under PRC law.
- Draft a contract framework for this transaction.
- Rewrite this clause and identify risks.
- Check signing and approval issues before execution.
