# Essays in Persuasion - Policy Diagnosis Quotes

Verbatim excerpts from *Essays in Persuasion* (John Maynard Keynes).

---

## germany-pays-in-goods

> "Germany can pay in the long run in goods, and in goods only."

*(The Capacity of Germany to Pay Reparations)*

---

## transfer-destroys-balance

> "If one country is required to transfer to another without payment great quantities of goods, for which this equilibrium does not allow, the balance is destroyed."

*(The Capacity of Germany to Pay Reparations)*

---

## inflation-and-deflation-injure

> "Each process, Inflation and Deflation alike, has inflicted great injuries."

*(Inflation and Deflation)*

---

## inflation-unjust-deflation-inexpedient

> "Thus Inflation is unjust and Deflation is inexpedient."

*(Inflation and Deflation)*

---

## deflation-provokes-unemployment

> "Of the two perhaps Deflation is, if we rule out exaggerated inflations such as that of Germany, the worse; because it is worse, in an impoverished world, to provoke unemployment than to disappoint the rentier."

*(Inflation and Deflation)*

---

## reject-prewar-gold-standard

> "I reject the policy of restoring the gold standard on pre-war lines."

*(The Return to Gold)*

---

## gold-standard-links-city-wall-street

> "A gold standard means, in practice, nothing but to have the same price level and the same money rates (broadly speaking) as the United States."

*(The Return to Gold)*

---

## economists-as-dentists

> "If economists could manage to get themselves thought of as humble, competent people, on a level with dentists, that would be splendid!"

*(Economic Possibilities for Our Grandchildren)*
