# The Most Important Thing — Risk Quotes

Verbatim excerpts from *The Most Important Thing Illuminated* (Howard Marks, 2013).

---

## risk-not-volatility

> "Risk is not volatility — it's the probability of permanent loss."

*(Chapter 5: Understanding Risk)*

---

## risk-invisible

> "Risk is subjective, invisible, and hard to quantify."

*(Chapter 5: Understanding Risk)*

---

## high-prices-create-risk

> "High risk comes from high prices, not from high-quality or dangerous assets."

*(Chapter 6: Recognizing Risk)*

---

## no-risk-is-the-risk

> "The most dangerous thing is to believe there is no risk."

*(Chapter 6: Recognizing Risk)*

---

## rising-prices-create-risk

> "Rising prices create risk; falling prices reduce risk."

*(Chapter 6: Recognizing Risk)*

---

## risk-control-not-avoidance

> "Risk control is not risk avoidance. The best investors take risks when they're compensated and avoid them when they're not."

*(Chapter 7: Controlling Risk)*

---

## biggest-losses-unimaginable

> "The biggest losses come from failure to imagine the unimaginable."

*(Chapter 18: Avoiding Pitfalls)*

---

## defensive-investing

> "Defensive investing — avoiding losses — is more important than aggressive investing — chasing gains."

*(Chapter 17: Investing Defensively)*

---

## outperformance-down-markets

> "Outperformance comes from doing things others aren't doing, especially when they're wrong. Asymmetric performance: outperform in down markets, keep up in up markets."

*(Chapter 19: Adding Value)*
