# Steal Like an Artist — Creative Principles Quotes

Verbatim excerpts from *Steal Like an Artist: 10 Things Nobody Told You About Being Creative* (Austin Kleon, 2012).

---

## school-yourself

> "School yourself. School is one thing. Education is another."

*(Throughout the book)*

---

## keep-all-passion

> "Don't worry about doing research. Just search."

*(1. Steal Like an Artist)*

---

## genealogy-of-ideas

> "Every new idea is just a mashup or a remix of one or more previous ideas."

*(1. Steal Like an Artist)*

---

## start-copying

> "Start copying what you love. Copy copy copy copy. At the end of the copy you will find your self."

*(1. Steal Like an Artist)*

---

## pretend-to-be-making

> "Pretend to be making something until you actually make something."

*(2. Don't Wait Until You Know Who You Are)*

---

## dress-for-the-job

> "You have to dress for the job you want, not the job you have, and you have to start doing the work you want to be doing."

*(2. Don't Wait Until You Know Who You Are)*

---

## identity-through-action

> "We don't know where we get our ideas from. What we do know is that we do not get them from our laptops."

*(4. Use Your Hands)*

---

## analog-first

> "Step away from the screen."

*(4. Use Your Hands)*

## productive-procrastination

> "The work you do while you procrastinate is probably the work you should be doing for the rest of your life."

*(5. Side Projects and Hobbies Are Important)*

---

## disconnect-to-create

> "Take time to be bored."

*(5. Side Projects and Hobbies Are Important)*

*(7. Geography Is No Longer Our Master)*

*(9. Be Boring)*

*(10. Creativity Is Subtraction)*
