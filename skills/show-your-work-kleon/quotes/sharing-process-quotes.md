Verbatim excerpts from *Show Your Work!* (Austin Kleon).

## today-taking-advantage-internet-social

> "But today, by taking advantage of the Internet and social media, an artist can share whatever she wants, whenever she wants, at almost no cost."

*(Source text extraction)*

*(Source text extraction)*

## daily-dispatch-even-better-portfolio

> "A daily dispatch is even better than a résumé or a portfolio, because it shows what we’re working on  right now ."

*(Source text extraction)*

---

## once-anything-copied-brought-contact

> "“Once anything that can be copied is brought into contact with the Internet, it will be copied, and those copies never leave.” Ideally, you  want  the work you post online to be copied and spread to every corner of the Internet, so don’t post things online that you’re not ready for everyone in the world to see."

*(Source text extraction)*

---

## going-give-damn-want-made

> "“No one is going to give a damn about your résumé; they want to see what you have made with your own little fingers.” Become a documentarian of what you do."

*(Source text extraction)*

---

## over-starving-artist-romanticism-idea

> "We all have to get over our “starving artist” romanticism and the idea that touching money inherently corrupts creativity."

*(Source text extraction)*

## traditionally-artist-been-trained-regard

> "Traditionally, the artist has been trained to regard her creative process as something that should be kept to herself."

*(Source text extraction)*
